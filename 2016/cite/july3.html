Arden — Before he moved to Buenos Aires, Argentina during the early 1940s, he lived in Uruguay and Brazil. In 1946 Arden moved to Paris and returned to Argentina in 1955 for a total of one year, and in 1956 he moved indefinitely to Paris where he died.[2] He has been a poet, political writer, painter, sculptor and co-founder of the international artistic movement "Madí".
